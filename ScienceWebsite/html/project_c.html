About The Project

The 'Harnessing Human Energy' project focused on developing innovative systems to provide a reliable mobile charging solution for a rescue team. Our approach involved harnessing human energy by converting kinetic energy generated from walking into electrical energy using a pressure sensor. This energy could then be utilised to charge devices without the need for a traditional power source.
